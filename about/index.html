My name is Andy Milenius, but you can also call me zandy.

I come from the shores of Lake St. Clair, which is a part of the Great Lakes Basin in North America. You can see it here in between Lake Huron and Lake Erie.

My hometown is a suburb of Detroit, which is a very unique place.

I'm best known for my work on MakerDAO, a shadow banking project based on the Ethereum blockchain. I worked on MakerDAO from 2016 until 2019.

Today I live in Brooklyn, New York. I'm mostly interested in ecology, mutual aid, history and geography. I search for free environments where a new way to live is possible.
